Welcome

Welcome to Sundown Tavern in Ruston, La. Sundown Tavern was established April 17, 1975. Owned by Bruce Van Zandt, it has for years been a local favorite. Serving up sandwiches, salads, and the best burgers in town (well, we're proud of them).

Sundown Tavern is the downtown lunch hot spot. You can find professionals, students, and families alike enjoying a bite to eat on our spacious patio. And don't forget to come see us after work!
